General

We know you care about your personal information, so we have prepared this privacy policy (our “Privacy Policy”). By using or accessing the app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.


Information we collect

We don’t collect any data or personal information from users. There is no login needed to use the app and there’s no way to provide any personal information to us through the app. Neither do we collect any data from device sensors like the camera or the users location. We don’t do use, save or analyse image data taken by the camera. The app doesn’t log anything to any server.
